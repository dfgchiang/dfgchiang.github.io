CDFW ADA compliance 8 fundamental requirements

  1. Use sans-serif fonts; keep body text to a minimum size of 12 points
  2. Provide text descriptions for non-text content (photos, diagrams, maps, etc.) in the form of Alt-text
  3. Use descriptive text for hyperlinks
  4. Ensure meaning isn't conveyed through color alone
  5. Maintain sufficient contrast between text and background
  6. Create content structure with headings
  7. Use simple data table structure with column headers; don't merge cells
  8. Use Accessibility Checker to check work
